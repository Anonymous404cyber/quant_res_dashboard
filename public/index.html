方法说明 Methodology

  • Training: Expanding window out-of-sample (OOS) training. 60 days minimum training window.
  • Model: LightGBM classification task. Targets: return_next_1d > 0, return_next_5d > 0.
  • OOS: Historical out-of-sample predictions, no future data leakage.
  • AUC & Accuracy: auc_all / accuracy_all are computed from all OOS records up to eval_date; auc_recent / accuracy_recent from most recent 50 records.
  • SHAP: Aggregate feature importance from latest full model training.

重要备注 Important Notes

  • pred_date: Each asset uses its own latest valid trading day (not a unified date). Bitcoin runs 24/7, equities follow their market calendars.
  • Current version: MVP 1.0. Historical predictions are read from app_oos_predictions.
  • Time windows: Current data range: 2025-09-30 to 2026-03-29.
